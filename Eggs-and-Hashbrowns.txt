Eggs and Hashbrowns

Chop half an onion and similar amount of banana peppers
Add extra virgin olive oil to pan on medium heat
Add onions and peppers, cook until onions translucent
Add diced hashbrowns
Flip occasionally until soft, then coat top in seasoning salt and mix it in
Continue cooking until brown, put in container

Lower heat of the pan to low
Add eggs
Cook until ready to flip, break yoke, then flip
Cook more until satisfied
Add pepper to hashbrowns then eggs
Cheese and hot sauce for extra flavour
